What is FarmCube Nano?

FarmCube Nano is a compact indoor grow cabinet designed for restaurants, cafés, hotels, education spaces and premium interiors. It supports fresh herbs, microgreens and leafy greens through aeroponic growing, LED lighting and automated nutrient delivery.

Project Questions

Common questions about FarmCube Nano.

Practical answers for system selection, installation, operation and project planning.

Who is FarmCube Nano designed for?Detailed product information

The Nano is purpose-built for front-of-house environments — restaurants, hotels, lounges, cafés, and premium homes. Its wood-grain exterior and transparent display panels are designed to be visible to guests, not hidden in a back kitchen.

What crops can I grow, and how often can I harvest?Detailed product information

The Nano supports herbs, leafy greens (lettuce, kale, parsley, and more), and microgreens using interchangeable 8-cell and 12-punnet trays. Fast-cycling crops like basil and radish microgreens yield up to 47 harvests per year; leafy greens average 16–18 cycles annually.

How much water does the system use?Detailed product information

The recirculating aeroponic system uses approximately 95% less water than conventional soil-based farming. Water is delivered as fine mist directly to root zones and returned to the 80-litre reservoir for reuse.

Does it require specialist knowledge to operate?Detailed product information

No. The 7-inch touchscreen features preset growing programs for different crops. The system is designed for non-specialist users — busy restaurant teams can operate it reliably without an agronomist on-site.

How is the lighting controlled?Detailed product information

Full-spectrum LED panels (400–730 nm) are dimmable from 0–100% via a 0–10 V analogue signal. Vegetative and flowering modes are selectable per tier, with programmable on/off schedules through the touchscreen or mobile app.

What are the installation requirements?Detailed product information

The Nano operates on 110–277 V / 50–60 Hz power. Water supply can be via an auto-fill pipeline connection or manual topping of the 80 L tank. The unit ships at 1,580 × 770 × 2,240 mm and weighs approximately 320 kg fully assembled.
