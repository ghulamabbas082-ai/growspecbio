Six Integrated Systems in One Kit

Every element of the Omni-4 is purpose-designed to work together. The following components are included in the complete kit as shipped.

Precision Aeroponic Buckets

20–60 μm Mist. Maximum Root Uptake.

The Omni-4's four aeroponic buckets suspend roots in air and deliver precision-size nutrient droplets (20–60 μm) directly to root hairs. This droplet range maximises surface contact with root hairs while maintaining high oxygen availability — outperforming deep-water culture and coco substrates for root development speed.

A 60 L reservoir with visible level tube and included water pump keeps nutrient supply consistent. Nutrient management is manual mode for full grower control.

Flexible Ventilation Control

Stage-Adaptive Airflow for Every Growth Phase

The Omni-4's adjustable exhaust duct system is engineered for the specific airflow requirements of each high-value crop growth stage. Swapping the duct configuration takes minutes — no tools, no rewiring. This is the key differentiator that separates the Omni-4 from fixed-vent growing systems.

Stage 1
Seedling
Standard Exhaust Duct
Gentle, reduced-pressure airflow prevents mechanical stress on young stems and root development. Maintains stable humidity and temperature to support rapid establishment without transpiration shock.
Stage 2
Vegetative
Shortened Duct Extension
Increased plant height and enhanced transpiration rates demand improved mid-canopy airflow. The shortened duct extension redirects air movement upward through the expanding canopy — promoting vigorous lateral leaf development and preventing humidity stratification.
Stage 3
Flowering
Upper Canopy Directional
Directs airflow through the upper canopy where flower clusters form — reducing localised humidity and heat stress around developing buds. Critical for preventing botrytis and supporting optimal terpene and cannabinoid development at harvest.

Product & Project Questions

Common questions about Pegma Omni-4.

Practical answers for model selection, installation, operation and project planning.

What size grow tent does the Pegma Omni-4 fit?Detailed product information

The Omni-4 has a 1 m² (1,000 × 1,000 mm) base footprint, making it compatible with all standard 1 × 1 m grow tents and larger. The frame extends to 2,155 mm total height — verify your tent's interior height accommodates this before ordering.

How does the aeroponic bucket system work?Detailed product information

Each of the four aeroponic buckets contains precision nozzles that mist nutrient solution at 20–60 μm droplet size directly onto exposed root zones. This droplet range maximises surface-area contact with root hairs without submerging them — sustaining high oxygen availability throughout the root zone and accelerating nutrient uptake compared to deep-water culture or coco methods.

Why does the ventilation system change between growth stages?Detailed product information

Airflow requirements differ substantially across the high-value crop growth cycle. Young seedlings need gentle, humid air with minimal mechanical stress. Vegetative plants transpire heavily and need more mid-canopy airflow to prevent hot spots and humidity build-up. Flowering plants require directional airflow through the upper canopy to reduce botrytis risk around dense bud sites. The swappable duct system enables growers to precisely match ventilation to plant physiology at each stage.

What is the difference between the standard and shortened exhaust duct?Detailed product information

The standard duct is used during the seedling stage — it maintains gentler airflow with reduced pressure. The shortened duct extension increases plant height clearance during vegetative and flowering stages, directing air upward through the growing canopy. Both ducts are included in the kit; switching takes minutes and requires no tools.

Is IoT and SaaS connectivity available?Detailed product information

Yes — IoT and SaaS integration is available as an optional upgrade. When connected, the system enables remote monitoring, data logging, and cultivation programme management via the GrowSpec cloud platform. The 7-inch touchscreen control functions fully as a standalone controller without connectivity if preferred.

What is the power requirement for the Omni-4?Detailed product information

The system requires a 220–240 V, 50–60 Hz supply with a peak draw of 900 W. The LED alone draws 800 W at full power. The LED input accepts 100–277 V AC, providing flexibility across international electrical standards. All power distribution components (power box, adapter) are included in the kit.
