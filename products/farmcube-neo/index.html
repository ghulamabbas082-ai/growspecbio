Purpose-Built for Microgreens

Throw and Grow — as Simple as it Sounds

Place seeds on the silicon mat inside a biodegradable punnet, press Start. The FarmCube Neo handles irrigation timing automatically. Harvest when ready, discard the punnet — no cleaning, no downtime between cycles.

Available in Black, White, Stainless Steel, and Wooden finishes in 2-tier (24 punnets) or 5-tier (60 punnets) configurations.

Tailor-Made Light Spectrums

Two Purpose-Built Spectra for Distinct Outcomes

FarmCube Neo features two engineered light spectrum programs. Each tier can be assigned to either program, allowing simultaneous cultivation of different crop types in a single cabinet.

Tailor-Made Spectrums

Two Spectra, Engineered for Different Outcomes

Lush (Pink) combines red (~660 nm) and blue (~450 nm) light to maximise photosynthetic efficiency — ideal for fast-cycling microgreens like radish and arugula.

Aroma (Icy Blue) is blue-dominant, promoting essential oil synthesis and richer flavour for culinary herbs like basil and cilantro.

Lush
Pink / Purple — Red + Blue dominant
Red light (~660 nm) stimulates stem elongation and leaf expansion for faster growth cycles. Blue light (~450 nm) prevents leggy growth and builds compact, dense structure. Optimised for maximum yield velocity.
Arugula Radish Kale Broccoli Beet
Aroma
Icy Blue — Blue-dominant spectrum
Higher blue-light proportion regulates stomatal opening for CO₂ uptake and promotes synthesis of essential oils and antioxidants. Intensifies flavour, aroma, and pigmentation in culinary herbs.

Project Questions

Common questions about FarmCube Neo.

Practical answers for system selection, installation, operation and project planning.

What is the Throw and Grow system?Detailed product information

Throw and Grow is GROWSPEC Bio's proprietary growing method that reduces the planting process to three steps: scatter seeds in biodegradable punnets, press Start, and harvest. Post-harvest, punnets are discarded (compostable) — eliminating the cleaning step entirely and enabling immediate crop turnaround.

What is the difference between the Lush and Aroma light programmes?Detailed product information

Lush (pink/purple) blends red (~660 nm) and blue (~450 nm) light to maximise photosynthetic efficiency, growth speed, and yield — ideal for radish, arugula, kale, and beet microgreens. Aroma (icy blue) is blue-dominant, stimulating essential oil and antioxidant synthesis for richer flavour and aroma — recommended for basil, parsley, and cilantro.

Which model should I choose — Neo-2T or Neo-5T?Detailed product information

The Neo-2T (24 punnets, 120 W) suits home kitchens, café counters, and lower-volume restaurant use. The Neo-5T (60 punnets, 240 W) is designed for central kitchens, high-volume restaurant back-of-house, and retail-facing display where a continuous, higher yield is required. Both share the same footprint and finish options.

What crops grow best in the FarmCube Neo?Detailed product information

The Neo is optimised for microgreens — radish (up to 3,120 punnets/year in 5T), pea shoots (up to 3,600/year), carrot, cabbage, and basil are documented in the datasheet. The dual-spectrum system also supports culinary herbs where flavour development is a priority.

Does the FarmCube Neo require specialist operation?Detailed product information

No. The unit ships pre-programmed with optimal irrigation timing. Users only set the light schedule via the 2.8-inch control screen. The enclosed, self-contained design requires minimal daily intervention — it is suitable for busy kitchen teams without growing expertise.
