Overview

Multi-Stage Crop Production in One Vertical Frame

The Pegma Germin integrates dedicated zones for every crop stage into a single mobile rack. The first layer serves as the mechanical and hydraulic hub; subsequent layers are differentiated for germination, propagation, and full-cycle leafy greens cultivation — enabling continuous rolling harvests from a compact vertical footprint.

🌱
Multi-Stage Architecture
Layer 1 houses the integrated water tank and oxygen pump. Layer 2 is optimised for seedlings, microgreens, and early-stage leafy greens. Layers 3–5 deliver high-efficiency leafy greens cultivation at full cycle.
💧
GS-AERO Ponics Misting
Ultra-fine, nutrient-rich mist delivered directly to root zones eliminates water submersion, maximises oxygen availability, and accelerates root development. Uses 99% less water than traditional farming and only 1/35 of the land area.
💡
Optimised LED Spectrum
AB30 full-spectrum LED bars (30 W each, 120 × 25 × 4 cm) are positioned above each growing layer. The spectrum is engineered to boost root initiation and vigorous early growth, with 61.2% red (600–699 nm) and 20.7% green (500–599 nm) distribution.
🔧
Ergonomic & Modular
Ergonomic working height minimises operator strain during seeding, transplanting, and harvesting. The modular tray system simplifies installation, cleaning, and reconfiguration between crop cycles.
⚡
Rapid Turnaround Cycles
Aeroponic acceleration of root development shortens germination-to-harvest timelines, enabling faster crop rotation and higher annual output per square metre of floor space.
🎯
High Germination Success Rate
The combination of precision mist nutrition, oxygen enrichment, and optimised light spectrum significantly reduces crop loss at the germination stage — improving overall production consistency and quality output.

GS-AERO Ponics System

Precision Mist Root Nutrition

The GS-AERO Ponics system delivers nutrients via custom misting nozzles — eliminating water submersion and guaranteeing precise droplet size for optimal oxygen delivery to roots.

GS-AERO Ponics

Ultra-Fine Mist. Maximum Oxygen. Zero Submersion.

Plant roots are suspended in air within each tray's sealed growing tub. Precision nozzles deliver nutrient solution at controlled droplet size directly to root hairs — eliminating waterlogging, maximising dissolved oxygen availability, and driving faster root development than any submersion-based system.

The closed-loop system recirculates unused solution, using 99% less water than conventional soil farming and only 1/35 of the land area.

🌫️
Precision Mist Root Nutrition
Custom misting nozzles deliver nutrient solution at precisely controlled droplet size directly to root hair zones — no standing water, no waterlogging.
🔄
Water & Nutrient Recycling
Closed-loop recirculation collects unused mist from the root zone and returns it to the reservoir, minimising waste and ensuring consistent nutrient concentration.
💨
Adjustable Inline Air Mover
Airflow can be tuned per layer to match the transpiration demands of each crop stage — ensuring stable humidity and temperature uniformity from germination through harvest.
📦
Sealed Tray Structure
The sealed aeroponic tray design reduces water evaporation and contamination risk. Modular construction simplifies installation, maintenance, and crop changeover between cycles.

FAQ

Common Questions

What distinguishes the Pegma Germin from other Growspec growing systems?

The Germin is the only Growspec product specifically designed for multi-stage crop production within a single unit. Its five-layer architecture creates dedicated zones for germination/propagation (Layer 2) and full-cycle leafy greens (Layers 3–5), with the mechanical hub in Layer 1. This enables rolling harvests — while one layer germinates, others are mid-cycle or ready to harvest.

What crops can be grown in the Pegma Germin?

Layer 2 supports seedlings, microgreens, and early-stage leafy greens. Layers 3–5 are configured for high-efficiency leafy greens cultivation. The aeroponic system and AB30 light spectrum are optimised for fast-cycling crops including lettuce varieties, herbs, and microgreens. Contact the Growspec sales team for a full crop compatibility list.

How does the water system work?

Layer 1 contains a centralised water tank, water pump, and oxygen pump. The pump circulates nutrient solution through dedicated supply pipes to each growing layer, where precision aeroponic nozzles mist roots directly. Unused solution is collected and returned to the reservoir in a closed-loop cycle — using 99% less water than conventional soil farming.

What is the AB30 LED specification?

The AB30 is a 30 W, 220–240 V AC passive-cooled grow bar measuring 120 × 25 × 4 cm. Its spectrum is red-dominant (61.2% at 600–699 nm) with significant far-red (20.7%) and green (17.4%) contributions — a formula designed to boost root initiation and early vegetative vigour without excessive heat output.
