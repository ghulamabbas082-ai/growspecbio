What is BioCube Modular?

BioCube Modular is a scalable controlled environment agriculture growing room system for commercial vertical farming. It supports leafy greens, microgreens, strawberries and regulated tall plants through modular expansion, LED lighting, climate control, fertigation and automation.

System Overview

The BioCube Modular Strawberry system is a Total Controlled Environment Agriculture (T-CEA) solution engineered for high-density, year-round premium strawberry production. Each standard fruiting room measures 17.25 m × 17.25 m (≈298 m²) and houses 12 G4-pro VAS-12T-5R aeroponic rack units across a 6.0 m ceiling height. The system operates fully independent of outdoor climate, eliminating seasonality and enabling consistent harvest cycles. Backed by 6 years of R&D and 6 iterative generations of multi-layer aeroponics rack design, the platform is GMP & GACP compliant and modular by design — start with a single room and scale to 8+ rooms with identical production economics per unit.

Drip Irrigation System — Overview

GROWSPEC's GS-DRIP system is a precision drip irrigation solution tailored for specialty crops cultivation. A pump delivers nutrients and water directly to plant roots with 90% efficiency, far surpassing traditional irrigation. Small precision emitters ensure even delivery, while a closed-loop recirculating mechanism reduces water and nutrient waste. High efficiency minimises runoff and evaporation. Ideal for substrate-based growers or regulatory environments requiring traditional growing media.

Project Questions

Common questions about BioCube Modular.

Practical answers for system selection, installation, operation and project planning.

What is the difference between the Aeroponics and Drip Irrigation options for High-Value Crops / Tall Plants?Detailed product information

The Aeroponics (GS-AERO Ponics™) system mists nutrients directly onto suspended roots using 99% less water than traditional methods and providing superior oxygen for faster growth — proven to achieve 44+ lbs per line per harvest. The GS-DRIP system delivers nutrients via small drip emitters to substrate-growing media at 90% efficiency, offering a more familiar environment that suits teams with existing drip experience. Both achieve full GMP/GACP compliance; the choice depends on your team's expertise and production goals.

Can the Leafy Green seedling racks be converted to microgreens production?Detailed product information

Yes. When propagation is not needed, seedling racks and seedling lines can be repurposed to grow microgreens or herbs without any additional capital expenditure. This gives operators maximum scheduling flexibility across the same physical infrastructure.

How quickly can a BioCube Modular farm be commissioned?Detailed product information

Leafy Green and Strawberry systems are fully set up in 8–12 weeks. For large High-Value Crops / Tall Plant facilities — demonstrated by the REDTOE project in Bangkok — GROWSPEC has delivered a 130-day greenfield-to-commissioning timeline for a 51-room operation. Concurrent site preparation by the client during manufacturing is key to achieving rapid timelines.

What digital monitoring and control tools are included?Detailed product information

All systems include the TechGrower™ platform: SaaS + IoT + edge computing (APU). It monitors 15+ variables (pH, EC, CO₂, light spectra, temperature, humidity), provides AI-driven predictive analytics to reduce operational waste by 15%, supports full remote control via mobile or desktop anywhere globally, and includes multi-camera live feed integration.

Does GROWSPEC provide after-sales support and training?Detailed product information

Yes. GROWSPEC provides comprehensive after-sales support beyond project completion, including detailed Installation Manuals (the AHOVO client independently installed their entire system using these), tailored SOPs, crop data packages, and ongoing engineering support. The REDTOE and Natopia projects both received turnkey solution support from design through commissioning.

What compliance standards do the High-Value Crops / Tall Plant systems meet?Detailed product information

The BioCube Modular Tall Plants system is designed to fully comply with GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) and GACP (Good Agricultural and Collection Practice) standards. Infrastructure includes air showers, quarantine zones, HEPA filtration, UV air sterilisation, and a sterile environment design. All three real-world case studies (AHOVO, REDTOE, Natopia) were completed to pharmaceutical-grade standards with zero PGRs, pesticides, or fungicides.
