FAQ

Common questions

What makes the BioCube Container a standout solution for investors?

Engineered to GMP and GAP standards, it's a fully transportable, adaptable chamber suitable for growing, propagation, trimming, drying and storage — with setup options for diverse crops to enhance scalability.

How does the BioCube Container maintain optimal growing conditions?

T-CEA technology gives precise control over temperature, humidity, CO₂, lighting and airflow, enabling year-round cultivation in any climate.

Can the BioCube Container operate efficiently in diverse climates?

Yes. In cooler weather, Smart Mode draws in external air to cut energy consumption by approximately 50%.

Is the BioCube Container cost-effective for commercial-scale operations?

Its space-efficient design, reduced water/energy/nutrient use, and high-yield capability make it well suited to scaling profitably.

Is the BioCube suitable for commercial strawberry production?

Yes — as well as leafy greens, microgreens, tall plants, herbs and medicinal plants, with scalability built in for space and resource efficiency.

How does the GS-DRIP Hydroponics system benefit cannabis cultivation?

It delivers 90% nutrient and water efficiency directly to the roots, reducing water waste and disease risk while supporting yield and quality across growth stages.

Should I choose Aeroponic (GS-AERO Ponics) or Drip (GS-DRIP) for cannabis?

GS-AERO Ponics mists roots directly for faster nutrient uptake and the highest oxygenation, on a smaller per-container footprint (196 buckets / 26.6m² canopy). GS-DRIP runs plants in fibre bags or pots with recirculating drip emitters at 90% delivery efficiency — a simpler, lower-maintenance setup with a larger canopy per container (252 sites / 30.4m²) and the option to scale into a modular multi-container farm with dedicated propagation, trimming and drying containers.

What's the difference between Single-Tier and Dual-Tier cannabis builds?

Dual-Tier stacks two growing levels in the same container footprint, roughly doubling sites and yield per container — the GS-DRIP Dual-Tier (CF-VD224) reaches up to 252 sites and 375kg/yr. Single-Tier runs one level per growing line, which keeps headroom taller (useful for larger plants) and is typically deployed as one container within Growspec's modular farm system alongside dedicated propagation, trimming and drying containers.
