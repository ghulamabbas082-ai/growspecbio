Common Questions

Common Questions

What does the indoor farming ROI calculator estimate?

The calculator estimates indoor farming investment, operating costs, crop output, payback period and long-term profit potential based on system, crop and market assumptions.

Can the calculator replace a project quotation?

No. The calculator provides planning estimates only. Final investment decisions should be confirmed through a GROWSPEC Bio project consultation.

What is aeroponic farming?

Aeroponic farming is a soilless growing method where plant roots receive water and nutrients through a fine mist. It is commonly used in controlled environment agriculture systems to support precise root-zone nutrition and efficient indoor crop production.

What is controlled environment agriculture?

Controlled environment agriculture, or CEA, uses indoor systems to manage key growing conditions such as light, temperature, humidity, airflow, water and nutrients. It helps growers produce crops in a more stable and predictable environment.
