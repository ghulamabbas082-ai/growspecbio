# GROWSPEC Bio

GROWSPEC Bio is a vertical farming solution manufacturer and controlled environment agriculture (CEA) equipment supplier. The company provides aeroponic vertical farming systems, container farms, modular indoor farms, compact grow cabinets, and vertical rack systems for commercial growers, restaurants, retailers, research institutions, schools, and facility operators.
